\caption{
Simulation of effective permittivity during silver nano-island deposition.
 Curves correspond to deposition steps (step 1 … step 1).
 Parameters: final thickness 2 nm,
 \(\alpha=1.0\), \(f_0=0.133\),
 sigma-law=gauss, \(\sigma_{\min}=1.20\), \(\sigma_{\mathrm{peak}}=1.60\).
 Detailed step-by-step values are reported in the summary.dat file.

Panels: (a) Real part of the effective permittivity (Re\,(\(\varepsilon\))) versus wavelength for each deposition step.
 (b) Imaginary part of the effective permittivity (Im\,(\(\varepsilon\))).
 (c) Evolution of the wavelength corresponding to the maximum of Im\,(\(\varepsilon\)) (red points) and the low-energy ENZ crossing (blue points) as a function of deposition step.
 (d) Evolution of the high-energy ENZ crossing as a function of deposition step.
